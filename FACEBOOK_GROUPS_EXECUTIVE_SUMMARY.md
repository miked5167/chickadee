# Facebook Groups Market Research - Executive Summary

**Date:** November 5, 2025
**Dataset:** 100 Facebook posts from hockey parent groups
**Analysis:** 83 posts with meaningful content

---

## Key Discoveries

### Top Pain Points Parents Have:

1. **Price Transparency** (14 posts / 16.9%)
   - Parents desperately want upfront pricing
   - Frustrated by hidden fees and unexpected costs
   - Asking: "What's the going rate for youth hockey?"

2. **Coach Discovery** (24 posts / 28.9%)
   - Hard to find specialized coaches (goalie, skills, shooting)
   - Need transparency about credentials and expertise
   - Want availability and pricing information

3. **Quality Validation** (10+ posts)
   - Parents fear "money grabs" and scam camps
   - Looking for recommendations and validation
   - Asking: "Is X camp worth it?"

4. **Geographic Search** (15 posts / 18.1%)
   - Need "near me" functionality
   - Willing to travel but don't know where to look
   - Want regional options beyond their immediate area

## Critical Features You MUST Include

### HIGH PRIORITY

#### 1. Price Transparency
- **Make pricing a required field** for all listings
- Show pricing ranges: "$500-$1,000", "$1,000-$2,500", etc.
- "What's Included" checklist (ice time, equipment, meals)
- "Additional Costs" field (tournament fees, travel, uniforms)

#### 2. Reviews & Trust Signals
- Verified reviews (email confirmation required)
- Overall rating (1-5 stars)
- "Would you recommend?" Yes/No
- "Verified Provider" badge (5+ years in business)
- Owner responses to reviews

#### 3. Geographic Search
- Map view with pins for all providers
- Distance filters (10, 25, 50, 100+ miles)
- "Near me" search functionality
- State/province filters
- "Popular in [Your State]" sections

#### 4. Coach Specialties & Profiles
- Detailed coach bios with photos
- Certifications (USA Hockey, Hockey Canada)
- **Specialties filter:** Goalie coach, Skills coach, Shooting coach, etc.
- Experience levels (Youth, High School, College, Pro)
- Availability calendar
- Direct booking/inquiry

### MEDIUM PRIORITY

#### 5. Camp Comparison Tool
- Select up to 3 camps to compare side-by-side
- Compare: Price, Dates, Ages, Skill levels, Location, What's included
- Highlight differences

#### 6. Team/League Directory
- Team directory with roster availability
- "Looking for players" badge
- Position needs (e.g., "Need: 1 goalie, 2 defensemen")
- Tryout calendar
- Contact coach directly
