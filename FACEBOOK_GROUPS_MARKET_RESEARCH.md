# Facebook Groups Market Research - Hockey Directory Insights

**Analysis Date:** November 5, 2025
**Dataset:** 100 Facebook posts from hockey parent groups
**Posts with Content:** 83 meaningful posts

---

## Executive Summary

Parents are actively using Facebook groups to solve problems that a comprehensive Hockey Directory could address:

- **"What camps are near me?"** → Need: Geographic search & filtering
- **"How much does X cost?"** → Need: Transparent pricing information
- **"Is Y camp worth it?"** → Need: Reviews & trust signals
- **"Who has a good goalie coach?"** → Need: Specialized coach profiles

### Top 3 Pain Points Identified:

1. **Price Transparency** (14 posts / 16.9%) - Parents want upfront pricing
2. **Coach Discovery** (24 posts / 28.9%) - Need to find specialized coaches
3. **Quality Validation** (10+ posts) - Fear of "money grabs" and scams

## Strategic Recommendations for Hockey Directory

### HIGH PRIORITY Features

#### 1. Price Transparency (Evidence: 14 posts)
**What parents want:**
- Upfront pricing for camps, coaching, leagues
- Hidden fees disclosure
- Payment plan options
- "All-in" cost breakdown

**Implementation:**
- Required field: "Pricing" for all listings
- Options: "$500-$1,000", "$1,000-$2,500", etc.
- "What's Included" checklist (ice time, equipment, meals, etc.)
- "Additional Costs" field (tournament fees, travel, uniforms)

---

#### 2. Reviews & Trust Signals (Evidence: 10 posts)
**What parents want:**
- Protection from "money grab" camps
- Real feedback from other parents
- Validation before spending thousands

**Implementation:**
- Verified reviews (email confirmation required)
- Overall rating (1-5 stars)
- Breakdowns: Value for Money, Coaching Quality, Organization, Facilities
- "Would you recommend this camp?" Yes/No
- "Verified Provider" badge (in business 5+ years, good reviews)
- Owner responses to reviews

---

#### 3. Geographic Discovery (Evidence: 15 posts)
**What parents want:**
- "Near me" search
- Regional flexibility (willing to travel for quality)
- Multi-location camps

**Implementation:**
- Map view with pins for all providers
- Distance filter (within 10, 25, 50, 100+ miles)
- "I'm willing to travel" toggle (shows regional options)
- State/province filters
- "Popular in [Your State]" section

---

### MEDIUM PRIORITY Features

#### 4. Camp Comparison Tool (Evidence: 6 posts)
**Implementation:**
- Select up to 3 camps to compare side-by-side
- Compare: Price, Dates, Ages, Skill levels, Location, What's included
- Highlight differences in pricing and offerings

---

#### 5. Coach Profiles & Specialties (Evidence: 24 posts)
**Implementation:**
- Detailed coach bios
- Certifications (USA Hockey, Hockey Canada, etc.)
- Specialties: Goalie coach, Skills coach, Shooting coach, etc.
- Experience level (Youth, High School, College, Pro)
- Availability calendar
- Direct booking/inquiry

---

#### 6. Team/League Discovery (Evidence: 47 posts)
**What parents want:**
- Find teams with open roster spots
- Understand team levels (AAA, AA, A, B, House)
- Tryout schedules

**Implementation:**
- Team directory with roster availability
- "Looking for players" badge
- Position needs (e.g., "Need: 1 goalie, 2 defensemen")
- Contact coach directly
- Tryout calendar

---

### LOW PRIORITY Features (Future)

#### 7. Community Q&A Section (Evidence: 29 questions)
**Implementation:**
- Forum or Q&A feature
- Categories: Camps, Coaching, Equipment, Rules, etc.
- Upvote/downvote answers
- "Verified Expert" badges (coaches, trainers)

**Benefit:** Builds engagement, keeps users on your platform, improves SEO with user-generated content
