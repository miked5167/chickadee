### **Priority 2: Major Strategic Decision**

#### **Hockey Equipment Section** - MASSIVE OPPORTUNITY ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

**139,400 searches/month** - This is 7.4X larger than your Training section!

**Pros:**
- ✅ HUGE traffic potential (139,400/month)
- ✅ High commercial intent ($0.99 CPC)
- ✅ Multiple monetization paths (affiliate, reviews, guides)
- ✅ Low implementation complexity (content-focused)
- ✅ Amazon Associates + specialty retailers
- ✅ Drives traffic to other sections

**Cons:**
- ⚠️ MEDIUM-HIGH competition (many equipment retailers)
- ⚠️ Not a "directory" - more of a content/review site
- ⚠️ Requires product expertise and regular updates
- ⚠️ Amazon affiliate rates are low (1-4%)

**Revenue Potential:** $3,000-8,000/month

**Strategic Options:**

**Option A: Full Equipment Section**
- Create comprehensive buying guides
- Review top equipment by category
- Comparison tools ("Best Hockey Stick 2025")
- How-to content ("How to size hockey skates")
- Estimated effort: 100-150 hours initially

**Option B: Lightweight Equipment Content**
- Add equipment buying guides to complement other sections
- "Best training equipment for power skating"
- "Tournament preparation gear checklist"
- Estimated effort: 20-30 hours initially

**Option C: Skip Equipment**
- Focus on directory model (your core competency)
- Stick to services (not products)

**My Recommendation:** **Option B - Lightweight Equipment Content**
- Add equipment content as blog posts to support other sections
- Capture some of that 139K traffic without major pivot
- Test affiliate revenue without huge time investment
- Can expand later if successful

### **Priority 4: Traffic Driver (Low Monetization)**

#### **Hockey Arenas & Rinks** - 109,100/month ⭐⭐⭐

**This is tricky:** Second-highest search volume BUT difficult to monetize.

**Strategic Value:**
- 🎯 "ice rink near me" (67,100 searches) is a **gateway keyword**
- People searching for rinks often need training, leagues, camps
- Could drive significant traffic to other monetizable sections

**Monetization Reality:**
- ⚠️ Rinks typically don't pay for listings
- ⚠️ Low margins on advertising
- ⚠️ Ice time booking is complex (most rinks have own systems)

**Recommendation:** **Add as FREE directory in Phase 7+**
- Simple database: name, location, hours, amenities
- Use it to drive traffic to Training, Camps, Leagues
- Don't expect direct revenue
- Value is in referral traffic to other sections
